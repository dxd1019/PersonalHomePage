About Me (not Daniel)

I love music

My name is Thien Huong Duong, and I absolutely love music. One of my all-time favorites is Declan McKenna's album Zeros. Its vibrant energy and thought-provoking lyrics inspire me every day, especially when it comes to decorating my website. Each track fuels my creativity and helps me express my vision in a unique way!

Zeros is a concept album by Declan McKenna that explores themes of survival, identity, and the search for meaning in a chaotic world. The album follows a character named Daniel, whose ambiguous role serves as a canvas for reflection on personal and societal struggles. Ultimately, Zeros is a journey through space and time, encouraging listeners to confront the challenges of existence and find purpose in an uncertain future.

Wow

“What do you think about the rocket I built?” howls Declan McKenna on the opening track of Zeros, marking a bold leap from his uneven debut. At just 21, McKenna has transformed from a teen prodigy into a profound storyteller, using this ambitious concept album to explore themes of survival and meaning amidst chaos.

The standout track, “Daniel, You’re Still a Child,” serves as a narrative Rorschach test, while the album’s intricate musical references—from Bowie to the Beatles—root his critique of modern issues like climate change and surveillance capitalism in the tradition of British protest rock.

Listening to Zeros not only inspires my love for music but also fuels my creativity as I decorate my website. Each track resonates deeply, urging me to express my vision in vibrant and meaningful ways.
